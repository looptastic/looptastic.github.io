Know your life cycles

Mark Twain said that “by the Law of Periodical Repetition, everything which has happened once must happen again and again -- and not capriciously, but at regular periods, and each thing in its own period, not another's, and each obeying its own law.”

According to scientific theory, a person's life is influenced by what are termed rhythmic biological cycles that affect their ability in various domains - mental, physical and emotional activity.

Physical: It regulates the human energy, strength, endurance, coordination of movement. Emotional: It controls the condition of the nervous system and mood. Intellectual: It regulates creative abilities of the individual.

Predict your mood

When it comes to biorhythms, each cycle has a unique period. The interaction of these cycles varies. For instance, the physical cycle lasts 23 days while the intellectual lasts 33 days. All cycles start at a zero value. All cycles begin on the day of one's birth in the positive part of the cycle. At the half way point through the cycle the waves again cross zero and move into the negative part of the cycle like a wave pattern.

Now you can check your biorhythm chart at your fingertips and start checking your life cycles, which impacts activity, endurance, level of immunity, cognitive abilities. Three main types of cycles are taken in consideration for deciphering biorhythm: these are physical, emotional and intellectual curves.
